## Recording checks

- Record at 1920×1080 or higher with the PowerPoint selection handles legible.
- Use captions and confirm audio before upload.
- Show the repository URL and MIT license on the final card.
- Do not say “pixel-perfect,” “general template import,” “cross-platform,” or “universal PowerPoint support.”
- Keep terminal waits accelerated but do not fake command results; use the release-tag outputs.
