# Provenance

`slidewright-inherited-empty-bullets.pptx` is an original synthetic fixture created and saved by Microsoft PowerPoint for Windows. The creation script derives it from the repository's original synthetic MIT fixture, inserts empty paragraphs into a native body placeholder, and leaves bullet formatting inherited from the PowerPoint master body style. It contains no third-party template, media, personal data, embedded font, or proprietary brand asset.

The fixture exists only to prove source-bound removal of blank inherited bullet paragraphs without changing non-empty paragraphs, other shapes, the preserve-only slide, relationships, masters, layouts, or the theme.
